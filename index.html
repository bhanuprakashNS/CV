Bhanuprakash NS

Assistant Engineering Manager
L&T Construction

NS Bhanuprakash is a post-graduate in structural engineering from IITH. He is currently working as Senior Design Engineer in Engineering Design and Research Center (EDRC), Buildings & Factories IC of L&T Construction at Chennai. He has worked in the operating projects of Prestige Song of the South, Prestige Jindal City, Prestige Finsbury Park, The Prestige City in Bengaluru, and tender projects of The Prestige City, Prestige Primrose at Bengaluru, Prestige Windsor Park, Prestige Pallavaram at Chennai, Prestige Bougainvillea at Noida (residential township projects), Prestige Skytech (commercial building at Hyderabad). His work is to analyze and design the High Rise Buildings in the above-mentioned residential townships. Besides those he has analyzed and designed Ramps, water retaining structures like Sumps and Sewage Treatment Plants (STPs) using various IS 456:2000; IS 1893(Part-1):2016; IS 875(Part-3):2015; IS 16700:2017; IS 13920:2016; IS 3370(Part-2):2009 and various design aids.
